## ğŸ¢ Types of Brand Partnerships

### **Type #1: Micro-Influencer Deals**
Small/local brands work with creators under 5K followers.

**Examples:**
- Local clothing boutique (R500 per post)

### **Type #2: Affiliate + Bonus**
Brand gives you affiliate link + pays you extra to create content.

**Example:**
- Luno: R100 per sign-up (affiliate) + R1,500 for creating 3 promotional videos

---

### **Type #3: Product Seeding**
Brand sends free product. You review it (no payment, but free stuff).

**Why do this:**
- Builds portfolio
- Relationship with brand (leads to paid deals later)
- Content for your page

---

### **Type #4: Retainer Partnership**
Monthly agreement. You post X times per month for consistent income.

**Example:**
- R3,000/month to post 4 videos featuring their products
- Steady income, easier planning

## ğŸ¯ How Brand Deals Work (Process)

### **Step 1: You Pitch (or they find you)**
Email/DM brands you love

### **Step 2: Negotiation**
Discuss deliverables, timeline, payment

### **Step 3: Agreement**
Simple contract (what you'll create, when, for how much)

### **Step 4: Content Creation**
You create the content (stays true to your style)

### **Step 5: Brand Approval**
They review before you post (minor edits if needed)

### **Step 6: You Post**
Content goes live on your page

### **Step 7: Payment**
You get paid (usually within 7-30 days)

### **Step 8: Report Performance**
Send them screenshot of views/engagement
