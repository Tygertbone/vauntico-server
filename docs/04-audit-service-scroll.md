## What the Oracle Reveals

### 🔍 The Tri-Pillar Methodology

True audits don't just scan. They **illuminate.**

#### Pillar 1: Technical Health

_"Does this codebase honor the craft?"_

- **Architecture Assessment:** Is the foundation sound or fracturing?
- **Code Quality Metrics:** Maintainability, complexity, duplication
- **Dependency Audit:** Outdated packages, security vulnerabilities, bloat
- **Performance Profiling:** Load times, bundle sizes, runtime efficiency
- **Git Archaeology:** Commit patterns, technical debt accumulation, team velocity
- **Test Coverage:** What's protected, what's naked to regression

**Output:** Technical Health Score (0-100) + Priority Roadmap

---

#### Pillar 2: SEO & Discoverability

_"Can the world find this work?"_

- **On-Page SEO:** Meta tags, headers, semantic structure
- **Performance SEO:** Core Web Vitals, mobile-friendliness
- **Content Analysis:** Keyword optimization, readability, semantic depth
- **Link Profile:** Internal linking, external authority signals
- **Indexability:** Crawl errors, robots.txt, sitemap health
- **Competitive Positioning:** How you rank vs. rivals in your niche

**Output:** SEO Score (0-100) + Quick Win List

---

#### Pillar 3: Conversion Mechanics

_"Does this system turn visitors into believers?"_

- **User Flow Analysis:** Where do people drop off?
- **CTA Optimization:** Are calls-to-action clear, compelling, positioned right?
- **Form Friction:** Are you asking too much, too soon?
- **Trust Signals:** Social proof, testimonials, guarantees—are they present?
- **Mobile Experience:** Does mobile convert or confuse?
- **Loading Psychology:** Do slow pages kill conversions before they start?

**Output:** Conversion Score (0-100) + A/B Test Suggestions
