## ğŸ¤ The Negotiation Framework

### **Step 1: Anchor High**
Start 20-30% above your minimum acceptable price.

**Your minimum:** R600  
**Your ask:** R800  
**Likely result:** R700-800

---

### **Step 2: Justify Your Price**
Never just say a number. Explain WHY.

**Bad:** "R800"  
**Good:** "R800, which reflects my [X] engaged followers, [Y]% engagement rate, and proven results driving sales for similar brands."

---

### **Step 3: Show Flexibility**
Don't be rigid. Offer options.

**Option A:** Full deliverable at full price  
**Option B:** Reduced deliverable at their budget  
**Option C:** Payment plan / trade

---

### **Step 4: Know Your Walk-Away Number**
Decide BEFORE negotiating: What's your minimum?

**Example:**
- Ideal: R1,000
- Acceptable: R700
- Walk away: Anything under R500

**If they offer R400:** Politely decline. Your time is worth more.

---

### **Step 5: Get It In Writing**
**Always. No exceptions.**

After verbal agreement:
```
"Great! Let me send over a brief agreement to confirm:

- Deliverables: [X]
- Timeline: [Y]
- Payment: R[Z]
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% upon posting

Reply to confirm and we'll get started!"
```
