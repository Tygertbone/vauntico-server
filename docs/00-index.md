## üéì The Vauntico Voice Guide

### Tone Principles
1. **Mythic, not mystical** ‚Äî Epic but grounded
2. **Empowering, not preachy** ‚Äî Inspire, don't lecture
3. **Specific, not vague** ‚Äî Metrics, stories, real names
4. **Legacy-focused, not hype-driven** ‚Äî Build for generations

### Words to Use Often
- Forge, ritual, scroll, lore, covenant
- Ascension, transformation, empire, legacy
- Sacred, enshrined, artifact, vault
- Ownership, independence, sovereignty

### Words to Avoid
- Disrupt, revolutionize, game-changer (overused)
- Leverage, synergy, ecosystem (corpo-speak)
- Easy, simple, effortless (diminishes craft)
- Free forever, unlimited (unsustainable promises)

### When in Doubt
Ask: "Would this sentence survive 100 years?"  
If not, make it more mythic.
