# The Syndication Calendar
## 90-Day Content Ritual for Vauntico Launch

---

## Philosophy: Teach, Don't Pitch

**Every piece of content should:**
1. Educate first, sell second
2. Be standalone valuable (no paywall required)
3. Reinforce mythic positioning
4. Drive organic discovery

---

## Post-Launch: 90-Day Content Engine

### Weekly Rhythm

**Monday: Educational Thread (Twitter)**
Topics rotate:
- Week 1: CLI command deep dive
- Week 2: Workshop design principle
- Week 3: Audit methodology breakdown
- Week 4: Credit economy tip
- Repeat cycle

**Wednesday: Blog Post (1000-2000 words)**
Themes:
- Tutorial: "How to [achieve outcome] with Vauntico"
- Case study: Feature user transformation story
- Philosophy: Thought leadership on creator economy
- Behind-the-scenes: Product development, decisions made

**Thursday: Short-Form Video (60-90s)**
Platforms: Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, TikTok (if audience fits)
Content:
- Quick wins
- Feature highlights
- User testimonials
- Myth-busting ("You don't need $50k to build infrastructure")

**Friday: Community Engagement**
- Discord office hours (30 min)
- Reply to every comment/DM from the week
- Feature community creations

**Sunday: Newsletter (Bi-weekly)**
Format:
- One big idea (educational)
- One user win (social proof)
- One tip/hack (immediate value)
- One product update (sell subtly)

## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Twitter/X
**Goal:** Thought leadership + community

**Tactics:**
- Daily engagement (reply to 10 accounts in niche)
- 3-5 tweets/threads per week
- Amplify user wins
- Live commentary on industry news

**Tone:** Mythic but accessible, no corpo-speak

---

### LinkedIn
**Goal:** B2B credibility (agencies, consultants)

**Tactics:**
- 2 long-form posts/week
- Case studies with metrics
- Behind-the-scenes of building
- Engage with SaaS/creator economy posts

**Tone:** Professional mythic (epic but boardroom-appropriate)

---

### YouTube
**Goal:** Evergreen tutorials + brand depth

**Tactics:**
- Weekly tutorial (10-15 min)
- Monthly deep dive (30+ min)
- Shorts throughout week

**Series Ideas:**
- "Vauntico 101" (getting started)
- "Build With Me" (live project walkthroughs)
- "Scroll Narration" (read mythic scrolls dramatically)

**Tone:** Patient teacher with mythic flair

---

### Blog (SEO Focus)
**Goal:** Organic search traffic

**Keyword Targets:**
- "How to build landing page without code"
- "Best course creation tools 2025"
- "Website audit checklist"
- "Credit-based pricing for SaaS"
- "White-label workshop platform"

**Structure:**
- Problem (search intent)
- Education (build authority)
- Solution (introduce Vauntico naturally)
- CTA (soft: "Try free trial")

---

### Discord

## Content Repurposing Matrix

**One blog post becomes:**
1. Twitter thread (key points)
2. LinkedIn post (first 300 words + link)
3. Email newsletter segment
4. YouTube script
5. Carousel post (Instagram/LinkedIn)
6. Audio narration (podcast clip)

**One tutorial video becomes:**
1. Blog post (transcript + screenshots)
2. 5-10 short clips (Twitter/TikTok)
3. Email sequence (one lesson per email)
4. Twitter thread (steps as tweets)

**Efficiency:** Create once, syndicate everywhere.
