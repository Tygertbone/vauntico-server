## Affiliate Activation Playbook

### Week 1: Foundation
**Objective:** Understand, experience, document.

**Actions:**
1. **Use Vauntico for 7 days**
   - Generate 3 landing pages
   - Create 1 workshop outline
   - Run 1 audit (on your own site)

2. **Document Your Journey**
   - Screenshot key moments
   - Note "aha" insights
   - Record before/after metrics

3. **Craft Your Angle**
   - What problem did Vauntico solve for YOU?
   - Which feature resonated most?
   - What's your unique take on creator empowerment?

### Week 2: Content Creation
**Objective:** Build your affiliate asset library.

**Content Types:**
1. **The Origin Story Post**
   - Your creator journey + where Vauntico fits
   - Authentic, vulnerable, specific

2. **The Tutorial/Demo**
   - Walkthrough of your favorite feature
   - Screen recording + narration

3. **The Case Study**
   - Before Vauntico / After Vauntico
   - Quantify the transformation (time saved, revenue gained)

4. **The Comparison Post**
   - "I've tried [competitors]. Here's why Vauntico is different."
   - Fair, factual, respectful

### Week 3-4: Distribution & Optimization
**Objective:** Share, analyze, iterate.

**Channels:**
- **Your Blog:** Long-form deep dive
- **Twitter/X:** Thread-style breakdown
- **LinkedIn:** Professional creator audience
- **YouTube:** If video is your medium
- **Newsletter:** To your most engaged subscribers

**Optimization:**
- Track which content drives clicks (use UTM parameters)
- Note which angles get most engagement
- Double down on what works
