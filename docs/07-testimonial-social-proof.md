## Handling Negative Feedback Publicly

### The Vauntico Response Framework

**When someone posts criticism:**

1. **Acknowledge Truth**
*"You're rightâ€”[specific issue] isn't working as smoothly as it should."*

2. **Provide Context**
*"Here's what's happening on our end: [technical/process explanation]."*

3. **Commit to Fix**
*"We're prioritizing this. Expected fix: [timeframe]. I'll update you personally."*

4. **Follow Through Publicly**
*[72 hours later] "Update: [Issue] is resolved. Here's what we changed: [link to changelog]."*

5. **Thank Them**
*"Thank you for the feedback. It made Vauntico better for everyone."*

**Result:** Critics become advocates when you honor their truth.
