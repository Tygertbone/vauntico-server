## üé¨ Tactic #1: Strategic Duets (TikTok/Instagram)

### **What is a Duet?**
Your video plays side-by-side with another creator's video

### **How to duet:**
**TikTok:**
1. Find a viral video in your niche
2. Tap "Share" ‚Üí "Duet"
3. Film your reaction/response
4. Post with caption crediting original creator

**Instagram:**
- Instagram doesn't have "duet" feature
- Use "Remix" on Reels (same concept)

### **Strategic duet formulas:**

**Formula #1: The Agreement**
- Duet a video you agree with
- Add: "YES! And here's another tip..."
- Adds value to original video
- Original creator's audience sees you as helpful

**Formula #2: The Disagreement**
- Duet a video with a controversial take
- Add: "I respectfully disagree. Here's why..."
- Sparks debate = high engagement = algorithm push

**Formula #3: The Addition**
- Duet a tutorial/tips video
- Add: "Great tips! I'd add #6: [your tip]"
- Shows expertise, complements original

**Formula #4: The Reaction**
- Duet a shocking/impressive video
- React genuinely: "WAIT, THIS IS GENIUS!"
- Emotion = shares

## üìå Tactic #2: Strategic Stitches (TikTok)

### **What is a Stitch?**
You use the first 5 seconds of someone's video, then add your own

### **How to stitch:**
1. Find a video with a hook/question
2. Tap "Share" ‚Üí "Stitch"
3. Their first 5 seconds play, then YOUR response
4. Post

### **Strategic stitch formulas:**

**Formula #1: Answer Their Question**
- Original video: "How do you make money online?"
- Your stitch: "I'll show you! Here's how I make R2K/month..."

**Formula #2: Add the Missing Piece**
- Original video: "3 ways to grow on TikTok"
- Your stitch: "Great tips! But they forgot the MOST important one..."

**Formula #3: Debunk Misinformation**
- Original video: Bad advice
- Your stitch: "This is dangerous advice. Here's the truth..."

**Formula #4: Build On Success**
- Original video: Viral success story
- Your stitch: "This worked for them. Here's how I got similar results..."

## ü§ù Tactic #3: Direct Collaborations

### **What it is:**
Partnering with a creator of similar size for mutual growth

### **How to find collab partners:**

**Step 1: Find creators in your niche**
- Search your niche hashtags
- Look for creators with 500-5,000 followers (similar to you)
- Check if they respond to comments (active = good partner)

**Step 2: Engage first**
- Comment on their videos (2-3 times)
- Share their content
- Build rapport (don't just DM asking for collab immediately)

**Step 3: Pitch collaboration**
DM template:
```
Hey [Name]! I'm [Your name], also creating [niche] content.

I love your video about [specific video]. The [specific detail] was üî•

I'm building a community of African [niche] creators. Want to collab?

Ideas:
- We could each make a video shouting out the other
- Or do a Q&A together
- Or challenge each other

Let me know if you're interested! üôå
```

---

### **Collab formats:**

**Format #1: The Shoutout Exchange**
- You: "3 creators you MUST follow for [niche]" ‚Üí include them
- Them: Same for you
- Both audiences see both creators

**Format #2: The Challenge**
- Start a challenge: "#7DayMoneyChallenge"
- Tag other creators to join
- Everyone posts daily using the hashtag
- Cross-pollination of audiences

**Format #3: The Q&A Swap**
- They answer YOUR audience's questions
- You answer THEIR audience's questions
- Post to both profiles

**Format #4: The Takeover**
- They film a video for YOUR account
- You film a video for THEIR account
- Fresh perspective for both audiences

## üåç Tactic #4: Community Collaboration

### **What it is:**
Building a group of creators who support each other

### **How to build your creator squad:**

**Step 1: Create a WhatsApp/Telegram group**
- "African [Niche] Creators"
- Invite 5-10 creators (similar size to you)

**Step 2: Set group rules**
- When you post, share link in group
- Everyone engages (like, comment, share) in first 30 min
- Genuine support, no spam

**Step 3: Weekly wins/advice sharing**
- Share what worked this week
- Help each other with challenges
- Celebrate wins together

**Result:**
- Every video gets 10+ immediate engagements (algorithm boost!)
- Support system
- Networking for future opportunities
