## üéØ Upselling vs Cross-Selling

### **Upselling:**
Offering a better/bigger version of what they're buying

**Example:**
- They want: Budget template (R100)
- You upsell: Budget template + 1-hour coaching (R400)

---

### **Cross-Selling:**
Offering a complementary product

**Example:**
- They bought: Workout plan (R300)
- You cross-sell: Meal prep guide (R200)

---

**Both increase customer lifetime value (CLV).**

## üöÄ Upsell Strategy #3: The Bundle

**What it is:**
Package multiple products at a discount

**Example:**

**Individual products:**
- Budget template: R150
- Meal prep guide: R200
- Savings tracker: R100
- Total: R450

**Bundle offer:**
- "Complete Money System Bundle" - ALL 3 for R350 (save R100!)

**Why it works:**
- Perceived value (getting more for less)
- Higher cart value (R350 vs R150)
- Happy customer (savings!)

---

## üöÄ Upsell Strategy #4: The Downgrade Upsell

**What it is:**
When they can't afford main offer, offer cheaper option (still more than R0!)

**Example:**

**You:** "1-on-1 coaching is R1,500/month"  
**Them:** "That's too expensive"

**You (downgrade):**
"Totally understand! I also have a group coaching option for R500/month. Same content, just in a group setting. Would that work better?"

**Result:** R500 instead of R0 (they were going to walk away!)

---

## üöÄ Upsell Strategy #5: The Ascension Email

**Sequence:**

**Day 3:**
"How's [Product] working? Need help? Here's a quick tip..."

"Last chance! [Special offer] expires tomorrow. Want to take the next step?"

**Conversion: 5-15% ascend to next level**
