## üì± How to Create (Phone-Only)

### **Step 1: Choose Your Product Type**

**Beginner-friendly:** Start with a PDF guide/ebook
- Easiest to create
- Fastest to deliver
- Proven to sell

### **Step 2: Outline Your Content**

Open Google Docs, create outline:

**Example: "Budget Mastery Guide"**
```
Introduction (Why budgeting matters)
Chapter 1: Calculate Your Income
Chapter 2: Track Your Expenses (7-day challenge)
Chapter 3: Create Your Budget (Template included)
Chapter 4: 20 Money-Saving Hacks
Chapter 5: Stay Accountable (Habit tracker)
Bonus: Budget Template (fillable)
```

**Aim for:** 10-20 pages

### **Step 3: Write the Content**

**In Google Docs (phone app):**
- Write 1-2 pages per section
- Use bullet points (easier to read)
- Add personal examples
- Keep language simple
- Write like you talk (conversational)

**Time:** 2-4 hours

### **Step 4: Design It (Canva)**

**Download Canva app (free)**

**Create design:**
1. Open Canva ‚Üí Search "Ebook"
2. Choose free template
3. Copy your text from Google Docs
4. Paste into template
5. Customize colors/fonts
6. Add your branding (name, logo if you have)
7. Export as PDF

**Time:** 1-2 hours

### **Step 5: Create Delivery System**

**Option A: Google Drive (Free)**
- Upload PDF to Google Drive
- Set sharing to "Anyone with link can view"
- Copy link

**Option B: Direct WhatsApp (Simple)**
- Send PDF directly after payment

**Option C: Gumroad (Professional)**
- Upload to Gumroad.com
- They handle payment + delivery
- Take 10% fee + Paystack fees

## üí∞ How to Price Your Digital Product

### **Pricing Psychology:**

**Too cheap (R20-50):** "This must be low quality"  
**Too expensive (R1,000+):** "Too risky for my first purchase"  
**Sweet spot (R100-400):** "Affordable impulse buy, seems valuable"

### **Pricing by Product Type:**

**Checklists/Templates:** R50-150  
**Guides/Ebooks:** R150-300  
**Video courses:** R300-600  
**Bundles:** R400-800

### **Your First Product:** R150-250
- Low enough that people buy without overthinking
- High enough to be taken seriously
- Room for upsells later

### **The "Coffee Test":**
If your product costs less than 2 coffees (R100-150), it's an easy "yes" for most people.

## üéØ Product Creation Template (Use This!)

### **"The Ultimate [Topic] Guide for [Audience]"**

**Structure:**
1. **Welcome Page:** Who this is for, what they'll learn
2. **Your Story:** Why you created this (builds trust)
3. **The Problem:** Pain point your audience feels
4. **The Solution:** Your method/system
5. **Step-by-Step Guide:** 5-10 actionable steps
6. **Resources:** Links, templates, bonus content
7. **Next Steps:** What to do after finishing guide

**Pages:** 12-20  
**Time to create:** 4-6 hours  
**Lifetime value:** Unlimited sales
