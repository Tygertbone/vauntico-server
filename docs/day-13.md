## ðŸŽ¯ The Optimization Process

**Step 1: Identify Your Top 3 Videos**
- Look at views, engagement, watch time
- What do they have in common?

**Step 2: Identify Your Bottom 3 Videos**
- What went wrong?
- Weak hook? Too long? Wrong timing?

**Step 3: The Double-Down Strategy**
- Make 3 more videos similar to your top performers
- STOP making content similar to bottom performers

**Step 4: Test & Track**
- Try new formats
- Track results
- Keep what works, cut what doesn't

**Repeat every week.**
